# 3A — Decentralized Benchmark Validators

**Status:** not started
**Owner:** web3 + be2
**Target:** Weeks 20–26
**Dependencies:** 2A (attestation), 1A (benchmarking)
**Blocks:** 3B (fitness index)

## Objective

Open benchmark validation to independent operators who stake tokens. Multiple validators agree on benchmark scores for verified results. Decentralized "Consumer Reports" for AI models.

## Tasks

- [ ] **3A-1** Validator node specification — what they run, how they receive benchmark tasks, minimum hardware requirements — `web3`
- [ ] **3A-2** Staking contract for validator registration (minimum stake, lockup period) — `web3`
- [ ] **3A-3** Consensus mechanism for benchmark agreement (2-of-3 or 3-of-5 validator agreement threshold) — `web3`
- [ ] **3A-4** Validator reward distribution (per-validated-benchmark payment from protocol treasury) — `web3`
- [ ] **3A-5** Slashing conditions for dishonest validators (submitting fabricated results, collusion detection) — `web3`
